What is Red Alert?

Red Alert is a web based application targeted to assist State Farm Agents in communicating with their clientele. It allows Agents the ability to quickly select or highligh geographical areas on our interactive map, to send clients notifications.

What time do recurring automation send their messages?

Recurring automated messages are sent at 10 a.m. (Mountain Standard Time)

Where can users find additional information?

Additional Information can be found within our User Manual.
